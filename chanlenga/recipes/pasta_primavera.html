Pasta Primavera

Home

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Grated Parmesan to serve

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions; reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil, add vegetables and cook until tender-crisp.
  3. Toss pasta with vegetables, add reserved water to loosen, finish with Parmesan.
