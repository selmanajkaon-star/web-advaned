Chocolate Cake

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch pans.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients together. Add eggs, milk, and oil; mix until smooth.
  3. Pour into pans and bake 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  4. Cool, frost, and serve.
